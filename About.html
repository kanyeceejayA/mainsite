About CBS Group

Caribbean Business Solutions Group is a consortium based in Kenya managing diverse business interests in a wide range of sectors. The company is well positioned in the marketplace and is broadening and intensifying positions in carefully selected investment areas. Our business philosophy is to take active management roles in the companies in which we invest to vigorously add value and visible growth to the enterprises.

CBS-Group has a distinguished board of directors with a renowned wealth of Business experience. We play a major role in connecting small and micro business enterprises mainly owned by women and youth into the wider business community especially government business, sourcing and connecting our partners to opportunities, financing from within the consortium and ensuring unmatched delivery of services to the customers with our reputation for sound management along with awareness of investment and trading environments. All this is done on a shared revenue and services legal framework agreement model which is above board and acceptable to all our partners.
